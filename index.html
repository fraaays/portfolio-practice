TrafficEye

TrafficEye is a mobile application being designed to help citizens actively participate in road safety by reporting traffic violations in real time. By allowing users to capture photo or video evidence, submit detailed reports, and share some safety tips, and reminder through a community awareness feed, the app will empower the public to become the “eyes on the road.” It will also support authorities with a review system, online ticket issuance, and will ensure reporter anonymity, creating a safer, more transparent, and efficient approach to addressing traffic violations.

REAL’S FOOD PRODUCTS STOCKS AND MATERIALS MONITORING SYSTEM

In the fast-paced and quality-driven environment of food production, efficient inventory monitoring is crucial to maintaining smooth operations and ensuring product availability. Real’s Food Products, a growing enterprise specializing in snacks such as banana chips, taro chips, toasted pastillas, and dehydrated fruits, currently faces challenges in tracking the availability and usage of both stocks and raw materials. Without an organized system, employees often struggle with untracked supplies, misplaced items, and last-minute shortages that disrupt production. The absence of a structured stock and materials monitoring system has led to issues such as frequent unnecessary purchases, wasted resources, and increased transportation costs. For example, vital materials like plastic packaging, labels, and ingredients may go unnoticed until they are urgently needed, forcing the business to make repeated trips to suppliers and causing production delays. To address these operational inefficiencies, the Real’s Food Products Stock and Materials Monitoring System is proposed. This system will enable staff to monitor inventory levels in real time, reduce waste, avoid duplicate purchases, and ensure that all necessary materials are available when needed. With this monitoring system in place, the business aims to streamline its stock handling processes, minimize costs, and maintain consistent product output, ultimately supporting better decision-making and enhancing operational productivity.
